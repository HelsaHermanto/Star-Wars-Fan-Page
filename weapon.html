Standard Lightsaber

By far the easiest to use and the most common type of hilt seen in the galaxy.

Lightsaber Pike

Similar to the double-bladed lightsaber, the lightsaber pike featured two blades (yellow hued) at each end of a staff. It was used by the Jedi Temple Guards

Crossguard Lightsaber

The crossguard lightsaber was a type of lightsaber that emitted one or two additional, smaller blades on the sides of the primary blade. Variants of the crossguard lightsaber design included the forked lightsaber and the vented lightsaber

Shoto-style Lightsaber

Shoto lightsabers were miniaturized lightsabers. They were used by diminutive combatants like Yoda, whose stature made it impractical to wield a full-scale lightsaber, and also as off-hand weapons for those duelists who employed twin-bladed techniques, like Ahsoka Tano.
